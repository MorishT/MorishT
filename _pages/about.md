  <p><strong>A researcher in AI</strong> at Hitachi's Central Research Laboratory.</p>

  <p>I mainly work on <strong>natural language processing</strong>, including reasoning-capable large language models that help people make better decisions. I also study <strong>LLM-agent-based simulations of economic phenomena</strong>, such as economic growth and international trade. More broadly, I am interested in <strong>general machine learning methods</strong>, including ensemble learning.</p>

  <p>I enjoy formal academic disciplines such as physics, mathematics, economics, and symbolic logic.</p>
